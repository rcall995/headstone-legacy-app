Frequently Asked Questions

How do the QR tags work?

Each tag has a unique QR code. When scanned, it takes visitors to the digital memorial page where they can see photos, stories, and family history. You or your customers create the memorial on our website (free), then the tag links to it.

Can I pre-order tags without memorials?

Yes! We can ship "blank" tags with unique codes. The memorial can be created later and linked to the tag. This is perfect for keeping inventory on hand.

What's the turnaround time?

Standard orders ship within 3-5 business days. Rush orders (24-48 hours) are available for an additional fee.

Can I get custom branding?

Yes! Enterprise accounts (50+ tags) can add their logo to the back of tags and get co-branded memorial pages. Contact us for details.

What if a tag gets damaged?

All tags come with a lifetime replacement guarantee. If a tag ever becomes unreadable, we'll replace it free of charge.

What about laser etching directly onto headstones?

Perfect for shops with laser engraving capability! Our Digital License option gives you high-resolution vector files (SVG, PNG, EPS) optimized for laser machines. You etch the QR code directly onto the stone - no physical tag needed. It's a premium offering with higher margins since there's no shipping or inventory.

What file formats do digital licenses include?

Each digital license includes SVG (vector for laser machines), PNG (high-resolution), and a sizing guide. Enterprise accounts also get EPS format and API access for automated workflows.
