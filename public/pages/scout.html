Become a Legacy Scout

What is a Legacy Scout?

A Legacy Scout is a dedicated community member who helps map and preserve family histories by locating and pinning gravesites. Your contributions help build a comprehensive digital archive for future generations.

How to Get Started

Sign in as a curator, visit the Scout Mode page, and use your device to pin locations and upload photos of headstones. Our AI tools can help transcribe details to streamline the process.

Make an Impact

By joining the Scout Program, you contribute to a growing network of memorials, connecting families and preserving history. Your efforts ensure no story is lost.
