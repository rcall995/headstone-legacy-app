What is a Legacy Scout?

A Legacy Scout is a dedicated community member who helps map and preserve family histories. Your contributions help build a comprehensive digital archive for future generations.

How to Get Started

Sign in, visit Scout Mode, and use your device to pin locations and upload photos of headstones. Our AI tools can help transcribe details.

Make an Impact

By joining the Scout Program, you contribute to a growing network of memorials, connecting families and preserving history.
