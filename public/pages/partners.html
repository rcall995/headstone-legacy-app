Frequently Asked Questions

How does the referral link work?

When someone visits our site through your link, we track them for 30 days. If they purchase a QR tag during that time, you earn the commission.

When do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via your chosen method (PayPal, Venmo, or check) for balances over $50. Smaller balances roll over to the next month.

Is there a cost to join?

No! The partner program is completely free. There's no inventory to buy, no fees, and no risk.

Can I customize my referral page?

Top-performing partners can upgrade to co-branded landing pages with your logo and branding. Contact us after your first 10 referrals.
