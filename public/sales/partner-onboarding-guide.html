How to Present to Families

When to Introduce

1

During Arrangement Conference

After discussing the headstone/marker, naturally transition to "There's also a way to share their complete story with anyone who visits..."

2

With Memorial Package Options

Present alongside other keepsakes like prayer cards, memorial folders, or photo displays.

3

At Follow-Up Visits

For families who weren't ready initially, mention during aftercare calls or anniversary check-ins.

Key Talking Points

"More Than Just Dates"

"A headstone shows when they lived. A QR memorial shows HOW they lived - their stories, photos, and the people who loved them."

"For Future Generations"

"Grandchildren and great-grandchildren can scan this tag and learn about someone they never got to meet."

"The Whole Family Can Contribute"

"Multiple family members can add their own photos and memories. It becomes a living tribute."

"Built to Last"

"The tag is weatherproof stainless steel with a lifetime guarantee. The memorial stays online forever."

Sample Introduction Script

"Many families are now adding a QR memorial to their loved one's gravesite. It's a small weatherproof tag that anyone can scan with their phone to see a beautiful digital memorial - photos, stories, even a family tree. It helps visitors connect with the person, not just see their name. Would you like me to show you an example?"

Handling Common Questions

Q: "How long does the memorial stay online?"
A: "Forever. There are no monthly fees and the memorial is always accessible. The family can update it anytime they want."
Q: "What if the family isn't tech-savvy?"
A: "Creating a memorial takes about 5 minutes and is very simple. We also have an AI assistant that helps write the biography just by answering a few questions. And visitors only need to point their phone camera at the tag - no app required."
Q: "Will it damage the headstone?"
A: "The tag uses industrial-grade adhesive designed for outdoor monuments, or it can be attached with included screws. It won't damage the stone and is designed to withstand all weather conditions."
Q: "What if they already have a headstone?"
A: "The tag can be added to any existing headstone. Many families add it months or even years after the burial."
Q: "Can anyone see it or is it private?"
A: "The family controls the privacy settings. They can make it fully public, or require a password for certain sections."

Placing Orders

1

Family Creates Memorial

Direct families to headstonelegacy.com to create their memorial (free). They'll receive a Memorial ID when done.

2

Log Into Partner Dashboard

Go to headstonelegacy.com/partner-dashboard and sign in with your credentials.

3

Place Order

Enter the Memorial ID, select product(s), and enter shipping address. Choose drop-ship to family or ship to your location.

4

Track & Deliver

Tags ship within 3-5 business days. You'll receive tracking info via email.

Pro Tips for Success

  • Show, don't tell: Have a sample tag and pull up a demo memorial on your phone
  • Use the brochure: Hand it to families to review - many will bring it up themselves later
  • Bundle it: Include in premium service packages at a discounted bundle price
  • Follow up: Families who say "not right now" often change their mind after the service
  • Share success stories: "Another family just told me how much it meant to see their father's photos come up when they visited"
