# B2B Lead Sources & Prospecting Guide

> **Goal:** Build a list of funeral homes, monument companies, and cemeteries to contact
> **Target:** 4 new partners per month to reach 140 partners by Year 3

---

## FUNERAL HOME LEAD SOURCES

### Free Directories

1. **NFDA (National Funeral Directors Association)**
   - https://nfda.org/find-a-funeral-home
   - Search by state/city
   - Most funeral homes are members
   - Shows contact info, website

2. **Funeral Guide**
   - https://www.funeralguide.com/
   - Search by location
   - Reviews and contact info

3. **Legacy.com Funeral Home Directory**
   - https://www.legacy.com/funeral-homes/
   - Comprehensive directory
   - Linked to obituaries (shows active businesses)

4. **Yelp Business Search**
   - Search "funeral home" + city
   - Shows reviews, contact info, websites
   - Good for finding smaller local homes

5. **Google Maps**
   - Search "funeral homes near [city]"
   - Shows contact info, hours, reviews
   - Can see how many reviews (activity indicator)

6. **State Funeral Director Associations**
   - Each state has an association with member directory
   - Example: California FDA, Texas FDA, etc.
   - Often has member lists publicly available

### Paid Data Sources

1. **ZoomInfo** - $$$
   - Most comprehensive B2B database
   - Direct dial phone numbers
   - Decision maker names and titles
   - 14-day free trial available

2. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - $$
   - $79/month
   - Search by industry "Funeral Services"
   - Find owners, directors, managers
   - InMail for direct outreach

3. **Apollo.io** - $ (Free tier available)
   - Free tier: 50 emails/month
   - Paid: $49/month for more
   - Good for email addresses

4. **Hunter.io** - $ (Free tier)
   - Find email patterns for companies
   - Verify email addresses
   - Free tier available

### Industry Publications (for context/news)

- **Funeral Business Advisor** - https://funeralbusinessadvisor.com
- **American Funeral Director** - https://afdmag.com
- **The Funeral Leader** - https://thefuneralleader.com
- **Connecting Directors** - https://connectingdirectors.com

### Trade Shows (for in-person networking)

- **NFDA Convention** - Annual, October
- **ICCFA Convention** - International Cemetery, Cremation and Funeral Association
- **State Association Conferences** - Each state has annual events

## PROSPECTING WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Build Initial List (Week 1)

1. Pick a state or metro area to start
2. Use NFDA directory to get funeral homes
3. Export to spreadsheet with:
   - Company name
   - Address
   - Phone
   - Website
   - Contact name (if available)

### Step 2: Research & Enrich (Ongoing)

For each prospect:
1. Visit their website
2. Find owner/manager name on About page
3. Look them up on LinkedIn
4. Use Hunter.io to find email
5. Check reviews (activity level)
6. Note anything personal for outreach

### Step 3: Prioritize Leads

**Hot leads (contact first):**
- Have a website (tech-savvy)
- Active on social media
- Good reviews (busy business)
- Family-owned (decision makers accessible)

**Warm leads:**
- Have a website but dated
- Medium review count
- Part of small chain

**Cold leads (contact later):**
- No website
- Part of large corporate chain (SCI, etc.)
- Low/no reviews

### Step 4: Outreach Sequence

Day 1: Send initial email
Day 3: Follow-up email
Day 5: Phone call
Day 7: Second follow-up email
Day 10: Phone call
Day 14: Breakup email

## METRICS TO TRACK

### Activity Metrics (Input)
- Emails sent per day
- Calls made per day
- LinkedIn connections requested

### Results Metrics (Output)
- Response rate (target: 10-15%)
- Demo scheduled rate (target: 5-7% of emails)
- Close rate (target: 20% of demos)

### Pipeline Math
To close 4 partners per month:
- Need ~20 demos per month
- Need ~400 emails per month (at 5% demo rate)
- That's ~20 emails per business day

- Offer $50 credit for successful referral
